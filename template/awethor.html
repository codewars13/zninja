Awethor

As a startup enthusiast and the founder of Croatian startup conference Voogle, my relationship with startups does not stop there. Early 2015, few of my friends and me are starting Awethor.com - live text streaming platform for writers and authors. Awethor word in a coin from words 'Awsome' and 'Author'.
The basic idea behind the Awethor is for a writer to share his real-time writing with his audience.
We applied to Eleven Startup Accelarator (second largest Europe accelerator) and we made it to the finals with other 30 startups among more than 600 aplications.
